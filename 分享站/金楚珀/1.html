About MUN

我们的故事

微城未来模拟联合国社团，又称iSchool MUN。
我们旨通过社团活动及外出活动，锻炼学生的协作精神、语言精神、对外交往的技巧，提升学生的综合素质。帮助学生认识到社会，认识国际形势，为踏入社会做出准备。

iSchool MUN
We aim to train students' cooperation spirit, language spirit, foreign communication skills and improve their comprehensive quality through club activities and outdoor activities.
To help students understand the society and the international situation and prepare for entering the society.

我们虽然做为一个新兴起的模拟联合国社团，在经历了各种问题及危机后，我们一同通过了难关，来到了这里。
但是，在经历了一次次困难后，我们更加坚定自己的立场，决定做出实质性的改变。
我们对于各位的建议洗耳恭听，并希望诸位能够帮助我们。

As a new Model United Nations community, we went through all kinds of problems and crises, and finally made it through and came here.
After going through difficulties one after another, we have strengthened our position and decided to make substantive changes.
We are all ears to your suggestions and hope you can help us.
